Philippines Pearla

Pearla is the mascot of North America's whitetails team.The mascot pearla team color is same as body color of the deer that is light brown. White-colored stripes are made on its entire body.Deer is considered to be a symbol of good luck and fortune.

Fun facts about the Mascot

  • Deer can jump up to 10ft high and are very good swimmers
  • Male deer are called bucks
  • Deer can run up to 30 miles per hour to escape
